About Us

Shaback Logistic Company is a specialized logistics provider dedicated to the safe, efficient, and reliable transportation of coal from mining operations to Eskom power stations. As a trusted partner in South Africa’s energy sector. Our experience and expertise ensure that coal is delivered on time, every time, supporting Eskom’s critical role in powering South Africa’s economy. We understand the unique challenges of coal transportation, including the need for high-volume, long-distance delivery, tight schedules, and adherence to environmental and safety regulations. Our extensive fleet, skilled personnel, and robust infrastructure are designed to meet the specific demands of transporting coal efficiently and sustainably.
